Description: Depending on the button clicked, the user can either add or withdraw money from their account.
Use:

ElegirMetodoPago
Description: The gym offers two options for depositing or withdrawing money: using the saved card on the user's account or completing the transaction with a new card.
Use:
The user can click on the preferred payment method.
TarjetaGuardadaPagar
Description: The user can pay using the saved card. They can check the card details to ensure correctness, and update it if it's incorrect.
Use:

Tarjeta
Description: Fields for Credit Card Number, Expiration Date, and CVV appear, allowing the user to pay with a card not registered in the database.
Use:
The user fills in the fields with a new card and completes the payment.

Rishi Kalpesh Shah
Making a Reservation
Description: The reservation menu allows users to choose a service and schedule it. After selecting an activity, the user is prompted to enter a date and time. The user can then choose to pay by credit card or opt to pay at the gym.
Use:
Users interact with the service selection table, select a time slot, and choose a payment method.
Files:
ReservationMenu.java, ReservationMenu.css, ReservationEntry.java, Services.java
Make a Payment
Description: Users can pay for services using a credit card. The system validates inputs such as card number, expiration date, and CVV on the client side before processing the transaction.
Use:
After selecting a service, users are directed to a form to enter payment details. Upon submission, the service is marked as paid.
Files:
PaymentCard.java, PaymentCard.css, PaymentCard.js, PayForExistingReservation.java, AddReservationPaid.java, AddReservationUnPaid.java, ReservationEntry.java
View Reservation History
Description: This section displays the user’s past and upcoming reservations, clearly marking which ones are pending and which are paid. Pending ones include a button to complete the payment.
Use:
The user accesses the reservation history page to view and manage their bookings, including completing any pending payments.
Files:
ReservationHistoryView.java, ReservationHistory.css, ReservationEntry.java
